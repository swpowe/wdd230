Purpose:

The purpose of my site will to give quick and insightful weather reports to a variety of different users. If you're out and about and trying to plan your next adventure you'll be able to quickly see the weather where you're at and the weather at your destination. If you're looking to plan a trip and need weather along the way we'll show you the weather at all major points along your trip.
