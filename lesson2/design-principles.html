Repetition and Contrast

Apple

apple.com

I think this page shows a good use of both repetition and contrast. Repeating the phones on both the top and the bottom of the page as well as the lines of text in the middle as kind of mirrors of eachother. Also the contrast between the colors of the text and the sizing really make the iPhone 13 stand out. Each of the different colors for every bit of text makes it part of the whole but also stand on its own.

Proximity

Nike

nike.com

On this page proximity is what really stood out. The way the page is divided into sections and you have the top menu and navigation seperated from the store aspect at the bottom. You're able to school through different sections of the store and then see those results at the bottom. Bottons for filtering are close to the results allowing you to refine your search.

Rule of Thirds and Spacing

Spitfire Audio

spitfireaudio.com

I like how this page used the rule of thirds with the organ image as the background. Also using a gradient with the white space and simple text allows you to see the organ in the background image matching the library title Symphonic Organ.
